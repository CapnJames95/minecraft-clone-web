How to Play

Goals

  • Survive — keep your HP, hunger, and (questionable) nicotine in check.
  • Explore villages, vape shops, and dodgy shops. Each one's procedurally placed.
  • Trade items for vapes at neon-lit vape shops. Get the Flavour of the Week at half price.
  • Buy the Loyalty Card to make every future trade cheaper.
  • Beware dodgy shops — they sell sweets, fake fags, expired vapes. Buyer beware.
  • Don't overdo the nicotine or things go blurry.
  • Mine ores (coal, iron, gold, diamond, emerald, lapis, redstone), craft, and smelt at furnaces.
  • Sleep in beds at night to skip to morning. Don't touch the cactus.

Gamepad (Steam Deck / Xbox / Ayn Thor)

  • Left stick: Move   Right stick: Look
  • A: Jump   B: Close menu   X: Break/Attack   Y: Place/Use
  • LB/RB: Cycle hotbar   LT: Sprint   RT: Vape
  • View/Back: Inventory   Menu/Start: Settings
  • D-pad: Cycle hotbar

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD Move   Space Jump/Swim   Shift Sprint   F Fly toggle
  • Mouse Look   Left click Break/Attack   Right click Place/Use
  • 1-9, 0, Shift+1-8 Hotbar   Scroll Cycle
  • E Inventory   C Crafting   G Eat food   B Spawn boat   M Minimap
  • Tab Settings menu   Esc Close any modal
  • Right-click bed at night = Sleep   Right-click furnace = Smelt   Right-click shopkeeper = Talk
